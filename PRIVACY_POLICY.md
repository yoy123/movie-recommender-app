# Privacy Policy for Movie Recommender App

**Last Updated: November 4, 2025**

## Introduction

Movie Recommender App ("we", "our", or "the app") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our Android application.

## Information Collection and Use

### Data We Collect

1. **Movie Selections**: The movies you select and your favorites are stored locally on your device using Android Room database.
2. **User Preferences**: Genre selections and app settings are stored locally on your device.

### Data We Do NOT Collect

- We do **NOT** collect any personal information
- We do **NOT** track your activity
- We do **NOT** share your data with third parties
- We do **NOT** sell your data

## Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services to provide functionality:

### The Movie Database (TMDB)
- **Purpose**: To fetch movie information, posters, and ratings
- **Data Shared**: Your movie search queries are sent to TMDB API
- **Privacy Policy**: [TMDB Privacy Policy](https://www.themoviedb.org/privacy-policy)

### OpenAI API
- **Purpose**: To generate personalized movie recommendations based on your selected movies
- **Data Shared**: Only the titles of movies you select are sent to OpenAI for generating recommendations
- **Privacy Policy**: [OpenAI Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy)
- **Important**: Your movie selections are NOT used to train OpenAI's models

### Optional: OMDb API
- **Purpose**: To fetch additional movie ratings (if enabled)
- **Data Shared**: Movie titles for rating lookups
- **Privacy Policy**: [OMDb API Terms](http://www.omdbapi.com/)

## Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device:
- Movie selections
- Favorites list
- App preferences

This data is NOT synchronized to any cloud service and remains on your device only.

## Data Security

- All API communications use HTTPS encryption
- Your API keys are stored locally in the app configuration
- No user authentication or accounts are required

## Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app is rated for general audiences.

## Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:
- **Delete Data**: Uninstalling the app removes all local data
- **Clear Data**: Use Android's app settings to clear app data at any time

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
